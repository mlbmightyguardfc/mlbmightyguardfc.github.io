The Guards

We are the Pride of Malebitsa.

Mighty Guard FC is a local Kasi Football Team situated in a village outside Marble Hall. We aspire to inspire the youth of Malebitsa to work hard on achieving their dreams and goals - not only in soccer but in life as well.

Our goal is to develop local talent that can soon be seen playing for World Class Teams all around the world and to help the youth realize their dreams. We believe in progression over perfection.

"United we stand, Forward we go."
